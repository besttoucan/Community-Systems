About Genesis Community Systems

A practical path to modern core capability for U.S. community banks.

Genesis Community Systems LLC helps U.S. community banks find a more practical path to a modern core. The work is hands-on: talking with banks, running the Design Partner program, and shaping a way to run the core that fits how community banks are actually staffed and funded, with less cost, less complexity, and less risk at conversion.

The company is led by Ohad Savir, Founder and CEO, working with experienced people across banking, technology, and implementation to support the Design Partner program.

Relationship to Genesis Core Systems

Genesis Community Systems LLC and Genesis Core Systems LLC are separate Genesis entities with complementary responsibilities. Genesis Community Systems focuses on community-bank engagement and Design Partner program activity. Genesis Core Systems supports the broader Genesis core banking platform strategy, product development, and ecosystem relationships.
